Hello, I'm Nicole

Welcome to my portfolio!

My name is Nicole Carman, and I am a software engineering student and freelance web developer who is actively looking for a remote intern or entry level software engineering position. Thank you for taking the time to look at my portfolio!

I have been passionate about web development since the early 2000s. My experience is self-taught from years of creating personal projects and freelancing. In 2020, I decided to continue my education and major in software engineering. When I'm not working on university coursework, I'm supplementing my learning with online coursework and certifications. My anticipated graduation date is in 2024.

I have over 15 years of experience with HTML, CSS, and MySQL. I began learning C++ in 2021 as part of my university coursework. In 2022, I began learning JavaScript and Python.

In addition to web development, I enjoy reading, knitting, and playing video games. In April 2022, I founded Nerds Who Code, which is an inclusive community for nerds like me who code, or who want to learn how to code. We focus on networking, study groups, and collaborative portfolio projects.
